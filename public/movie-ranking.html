The Best Movies According to Brian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Transformers

This one is a lot of fun watching cars transform into robots and jack stuff up. Megan Fox is also really hot.

The Guardian

Featuring Kevin Costner and Ashton Kutcher, it is a harrowing story of Coast Guard rescue swimmers.

Up in the Air

I love travel and watching this romance drama with George Clooney and Anna Kendrick is phenomenal. Be ready for an emotional pull towards the end.
